Basotho Traditional Clothing

Thithana is made using the leaves of a flowering plant from the called tsikitlane.

Mokorotlo is a type of straw hat widely used and is the national symbol of Lesotho.

Seanamarena is a traditional blanket hand woven that originate from Lesotho.

Mose oa khomo is made from the cow's skin and woren by women who are married.

Kobo ea morena is a traditional blanket hand woven that originate from Lesotho is made from wild animals.

Thithana ea litolobonya is made fron maizemeal sacks and bottle lits.

Beadwork is made using beads and thread and can a leglace ,necklace or waistlace.

Seshoeshoe is a printed cotton fabric used for sotho traditinal clothing.

Mosetla is a type of strew hat with a longer tail.

Ts'ea is made for young boys also made of skins of animals.
